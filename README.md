# econ1626-ai-future-forecast.
This project is an interactive web essay developed for ECON1626 Assessment 3: AI Future Forecast. It explores how artificial intelligence could reshape the Australian retail industry by 2030, with a particular focus on supermarkets, large omnichannel retailers, retail workers, consumers, and small businesses.

The central argument of the essay is that AI will not simply replace retail workers. Instead, it will change the economic logic of retail by turning uncertainty into prediction. By 2030, AI is likely to influence inventory forecasting, personalised promotions, customer service, staff rostering, online fulfilment, and competition between large and small retailers.

The essay is designed as a public-facing forecast rather than a traditional written report. It combines economic analysis, future scenarios, policy discussion, data-driven insights, and interactive web design.
